How to Send a Message on WhatsApp

Step 1: Open WhatsApp

Tap on the green WhatsApp icon on your phone to open the app.

Step 2: Tap on a Chat

Select a contact you want to message from your chat list or tap the new chat icon to start a new one.

Step 3: Type Your Message

Tap the message box at the bottom and type your message using the keyboard.

Step 4: Send the Message

Tap the green send icon (paper plane) to send your message.
